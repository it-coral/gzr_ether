Getting started

What do I need to unlock GGid Items? {{status.ggid ? '‒' : '+'}}

Here's what you'll need to get started with GGiD:

  • A computer running the desktop Chrome, Firefox, Opera or Brave

  • MetaMask, a digital wallet specifically for your web browser

  • Ether, the cryptocurrency that powers GGiD
How to buy GZR {{status.buy ? '‒' : '+'}}

  • To login to our web application and purchase GZR, you will need to install MetaMask and send ETH to your wallet.

  • MetaMask will act as your login to our website, meaning you do not need an extra password to protect your account.
Installing MetaMask, your digital wallet {{status.install ? '‒' : '+'}}

  • To purchase GZR and unlock your Global Gaming Identity you will first need to install MetaMask, a digital cryptocurrency wallet for Ethereum. You’ll need to fund your wallet with ETH in order to purchase GZR.

  • Note: MetaMask is like your bank account -- make sure you don’t forget your password or lose your seed words.
